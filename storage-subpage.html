Why it matters in Dubai

Dubai's climate is brutal on furniture

Temperatures above 45°C and humidity spikes warp wood, crack leather, and grow mould. Every PurpleBox unit runs AC and humidity control around the clock.

🌡️

Temperature-controlled

Maintained below 24°C year-round. Prevents warping, cracking and adhesive failure.

💧

Humidity-controlled

Protects against mould, mildew and moisture damage — common in Dubai's summer months.

🛡️

Sealed units

Individual locks, clean corridors, pest control. Your furniture is the only thing in your unit.

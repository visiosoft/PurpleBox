Leave aisle space

Do not pack wall-to-wall. Keep a small path so you can access important boxes without unloading everything.

Stack smartly

Place heavy cartons at the bottom and fragile items higher up. Use clear labels on every side.

Frequently asked questions

What if I am not sure between two unit sizes?

Choose the larger size when in doubt. It improves accessibility and reduces risk of damage from over-stacking.

Can I upgrade to a bigger unit later?

Yes. You can upgrade when availability allows. Our team will coordinate a smooth move inside the facility.

Are units climate-controlled?

Yes, PurpleBox units are climate-controlled and monitored, suitable for furniture, electronics, and documents.

💰 Pricing

Storage is priced monthly, based on unit size. We charge a flat monthly rate with no setup fees, no admin fees, and no key fees. VAT at 5% is added to the monthly rate. You can see our starting prices on the Pricing page.

The minimum term is one calendar month. After that, you can continue month-to-month with no penalty, or commit to a longer term for a discounted rate.

No. You pay the monthly rate plus 5% VAT. No setup fee, no admin fee, no key deposit. If you cancel after the first month, there's no cancellation penalty either.

UAE standard VAT (5%) is applied to the monthly storage rate. All prices shown on our website are exclusive of VAT unless stated otherwise. A tax invoice is issued monthly.

🚚 Pickup & moving

Yes. We offer a pickup service across Dubai. WhatsApp us your address and a rough list of items and we'll quote you within hours. See our full Packing & Moving service.

Yes. Our team can pack, wrap, and dismantle furniture at your home or office. This is included in our moving service quote. We use professional packing materials — boxes, moving blankets, bubble wrap, and stretch wrap.

Pickup and moving is priced separately from storage, based on volume, distance, and services needed. WhatsApp us with your address and list — we'll quote same day.

🔒 Security

The facility has 24/7 CCTV throughout, electronic access control, individual unit locks, and staff on-site during operating hours. We do not share access codes. Your unit has its own lock — only you have the key.

Optional storage insurance is available. We can advise on coverage options when you sign up. We recommend insurance for high-value items.

Yes. All units are air-conditioned with humidity control. Dubai's heat and humidity can warp wood, crack leather, damage electronics and grow mould — our facility prevents all of that year-round.

🗝️ Access

We're open Monday to Saturday, 9am to 7pm. Sunday is closed. For early morning or late evening access, contact us in advance and we'll try to accommodate.

Only you — or people you authorise in writing. Staff cannot access your unit without written consent. Your unit has its own padlock that only you control.

📄 Contracts

As long as you need. We offer month-to-month storage with no maximum term. For longer stays (3, 6, 12 months), we offer discounted rates.

Give us at least 7 days notice before your next billing date. After the first month, there's no cancellation fee. We'll arrange a time for you to collect your items or request re-delivery.

📦 What can I store

You can store almost anything — furniture, electronics, documents, clothing, business inventory, bicycles, sporting equipment, household goods, and more. All units are climate-controlled so sensitive items like electronics and artwork are protected.

Prohibited items include: flammable or combustible materials, chemicals, explosives, illegal substances, perishable food, live animals, and hazardous waste. If you're unsure about a specific item, ask us before storing.
